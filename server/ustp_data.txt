The University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines (USTP) is a state university in the Philippines.
The Registrar's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building.
To enroll, students must submit their Form 138 and Certificate of Good Moral Character.
Library hours are from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekdays.
The vision of USTP is to be a nationally recognized science and technology university.
Classes start at 7:30 AM.
The Citizen's Charter details the processing time for transcript requests is 3 working days.
The IT building is adjacent to the Engineering Complex.
Este is a local AI kiosk companion designed to assist students.
The university president's office is on the 2nd floor.
