The University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines (USTP) is a premier state university in the Philippines, with its main campus located in Cagayan de Oro City (CDO).
The CDO campus is situated along C.M. Recto Avenue, Lapasan.
The university has several colleges: College of Information Technology (CIT), College of Engineering and Architecture (CEA), College of Science and Mathematics (CSM), and College of Technology (COT).

Enrollment Process for Freshmen:
1. Online Application: Apply via the USTP Admission Portal.
2. Entrance Examination: Pass the USTP Admission Test (USTP-AT).
3. Requirements Submission: Submit Form 138 (Report Card), Certificate of Good Moral Character, and PSA Birth Certificate to the Admissions Office.
4. Medical Clearance: Undergo physical examination at the University Clinic.
5. Interview: Some programs (like Engineering) require a department-level interview.
6. Enrollment: Proceed to the Registrar's Office for encoding and assessment of fees.

The Registrar's Office is located on the ground floor of the Administration Building, near the main gate.
The Admission Office is located at the Student Center Building.
The University Clinic is situated behind the Science and Mathematics Building.

Academic Programs:
- IT Programs: BS in Information Technology, BS in Data Science, BS in Computer Science.
- Engineering Programs: BS in Civil Engineering, BS in Mechanical Engineering, BS in Electrical Engineering, BS in Electronics Engineering.
- Science Programs: BS in Applied Physics, BS in Chemistry, BS in Mathematics.

Scholarships:
USTP offers various scholarships including the UniFAST Tertiary Education Subsidy (TES), CHED Merit Scholarships, and the DOST Scholarship. 
Working Student programs are also available through the Student Affairs Office.

Campus Amenities:
- The University Library is located at the 3-storey building adjacent to the Administration Building. Hours are 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- The IT Building (CIT) is the 4-storey building near the back gate, equipped with advanced computer laboratories.
- The Engineering Complex houses the various engineering shops and the Makerspace.
- The Science and Mathematics Building (CSM) is known for its modern laboratories and research facilities.

Student Services:
- For Transcript of Records (TOR) or Certification requests, proceed to the Registrar's Office. Standard processing time is 3-5 working days.
- The Guidance and Counseling Office is on the 2nd floor of the Student Center.
- Student ID renewal is handled by the ICT Service Center at the ground floor of the IT Building.

Vision and Mission:
Vision: A nationally recognized science and technology university providing the vital link between education and the economy.
Mission: To bring the world of work into the classroom through merit, talent, and technology.
